Project Overview

A minimal yet bold online portfolio for Luma Studio, a boutique photography team specializing in lifestyle and editorial work.

Services Provided

Brand Identity

Creative Direction

Web design

The Process

  1. Moodboard & Brand Direction – Defined minimalist, editorial aesthetic.
  2. Website Design – Bold typography, white space, and fluid galleries.
  3. Webflow Build – Optimized for fast loading without compromising quality.
  4. Client Training – Guided team on managing and updating their portfolio.

The Results

The campaign website exceeded expectations, generating both buzz and measurable results.

  • 40% increase in campaign sign-ups
  • Featured in 3 design publications
  • Strengthened Nova’s brand recognition across digital platforms
